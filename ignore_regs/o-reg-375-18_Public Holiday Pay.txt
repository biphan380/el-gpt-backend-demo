ONTARIO REGULATION 375/18

PUBLIC HOLIDAY PAY

Consolidation Period: From November 21, 2018 to the e-Laws currency date.

Note: This Regulation is revoked on January 1, 2019. (See: 2018, c. 14, Sched. 1, s. 28)

Last amendment: 2018, c. 14, Sched. 1, s. 28.

This is the English version of a bilingual regulation.

Public holiday pay

1. The following manner of calculation is prescribed for the purpose of determining public holiday pay for an employee under clause 24 (1) (b) of the Act:

1. The employeeâ€™s public holiday pay for a given public holiday shall be equal to the total amount of regular wages earned and vacation pay payable to the employee in the four work weeks before the work week in which the public holiday occurred, divided by 20.
